About Us

Café del Mural is the culmination of a lapsed engineer and three baristas mastering the art of coffee in a make-shift laboratory.

We want to introduce the world to a different way of drinking coffee. As such, we provide everything from a fresh take on the traditional cup of joe to a coffee-based replication of your favorite pint of craft beer. We aren't just interested in what coffee is, but what it can be.

We want to spread the culture of coffee. That's why we provide educational tasting and roasting experiences.
